# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiters, hiring managers, and engineering leads reviewing Nguyen Tran Phuong Duy for Full-stack Engineer roles. They need to scan experience, technical range, project ownership, and contact details quickly from a web page or exported PDF.

## Product Purpose

This project is a personal resume website and PDF source. Success means the resume feels professional, minimal, technical, and easy to evaluate within a few minutes, with the strongest recent full-stack/backend work visible first.

## Brand Personality

Precise, restrained, engineering-led.

## Anti-references

Avoid colorful technology badges, heavy icon usage, decorative sidebars, template-like resume blocks, loud palettes, and visuals that distract from experience and project impact.

## Design Principles

- Lead with technical credibility before decoration.
- Make scanning effortless for non-technical recruiters and technical interviewers.
- Keep color restrained and purposeful.
- Prioritize concrete ownership, architecture, stack, and impact.
- Treat the PDF export as a first-class output, not an afterthought.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Maintain strong text contrast, semantic document structure, readable print output, and no motion-dependent interactions. The resume should remain usable when printed in grayscale.
